Diabetes dramatically increases the risk of various cardiovascular problems, including coronary artery disease with chest pain (angina), heart attack, stroke and narrowing of arteries (atherosclerosis). If you have diabetes, you're more likely to have heart disease or stroke.

To treat diabetes and prevent these diseases, patients need to record some indexes related to diabetes every day. This website is designed to help patients record, store and view data about diabetes for a long time and keep alert of the data, whether over the upper limit or below the lower limit.

Depending on patients' symptoms, each patient will record some subsets of the health data time-series belo

  • Blood glucose level (in nmol/L)
  • Weight (in kg)
  • doses of insulin taken (number of doses)
  • exercise (step count)

These are all numerical data. Patients can write down some comments for each entering data if they want to record their states.

To keep patients motivated to enter data everyday, we also provide:

  • Clinician's support message to encourage patients to keep recording
  • If patient's engagement rate is more than 80%, we will award a digital badge and display it on the website
  • The 5 patient with the highest engagement will be displayed on the leaderboard. So don't lay behind! Keep entering every day!

Attention: All users need to be logged in to access the features stated above. And each user's account ID is registered by the attending clinician.
